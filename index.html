About Me

Hello and welcome to my webpage! As you can see from the teal heading above, my name is Mark.

When I am not making ground-breaking websites, you can find me at my job where I work with international college students assisting them with their immigration needs and giving them academic advice. When I am not at work, you can find me either rock climbing or training for rock climbing. When I am not rock climbing or training for rock climbing, you can find me making energy bars for my (very very very) small company. When I am not making energy bars, you can find me at my friend’s woodshop where I am learning to turn wood on lathes to make bowls. These activities took place in a much higher frequency before I began the UW Coding Bootcamp. Now my time mostly consists of a furrowed-brow gazing toward a computer screen, forehead in hand, while feverishly clicking boxes in Chrome Inspector trying to figure out why my content won’t align.

Now that you know me better, please check out my portfolio page which I am sure will blow your mind. I recommend sitting down before clicking the link because the sheer awe of it may make your knees buckle. I apologize in advance.
